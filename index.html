Card Payment

These are the steps to use credit card :

  1. Customer will purches something
  2. Swipe credit card through a secured credit card terminal
  3. Now credit card data will be transmitted for approval as the terminal POS system is connected to the processing network
  4. Now you can approve or decline the transaction responding
  5. If you have approved transaction the processor and merchant receive an authorization response
  6. Now merchant completes the transactions
  7. Now the transactions that have been processed on that day will be close with batch closer
  8. The processors acquiring bank then collects the funds from the credit card issuers
  9. The processors aquring bank then deposites the funds into the murchent's business accounts

Mobile App Transaction

These are the steps to use GOOGLE PAY :

  1. Open the Google play, store and download the 'Google Pay app'
  2. Then select your preferred language and tap on the arrow (Placed in the right corner)
  3. Enter your phone number registered with the bank account (Allow your SMS, contacts and location)
  4. After that, it will automatically detect your email id, click on 'Continue'
  5. A one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your mobile number, let the app access it manually.
  6. After that, you have to choose the screen lock or you can create a Google pin. Select the option and click on 'Continue'.
  7. Set up your Google screen lock or pin.
